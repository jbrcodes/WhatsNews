About

This website displays a random selection of article summaries, translated into English, of a few selected foreign-language news websites. Summaries are updated automatically a few times a week.

Click on the 'show' button to the right of each website name to display the original foreign language title and summary.

For more information, see A Glimpse into Foreign-Language News.
