Intro

There are loads of news sources out there. And it's healthy to not rely too much on one source. The idea came from (kind of) a site called Watching America, where volunteers translate articles about the US in foreign websites. What's New(s)? is different in that the articles are typically about the country itself, and only the summaries of the articles are translated, using Artificial Intelligence (AI).

What It Does

...reads RSS files from websites, translates to EN

A Few Notes

  • They are not the most important articles. They seem to be random. They are possibly the most recently-published articles.
  • Artificial Intelligence does the translations. AI works great for quick stuff. For more subtle translations, humans still do a better job.
